About Me

I am a versatile and creative person. I have practiced equestrian sports, swimming, acrobatics, and aikido, which helped me develop discipline, endurance, and self-control. I plan to apply to an architecture program because I am interested in form, space, and visual thinking. I enjoy creating things by hand, including decorative flowers made from different materials. I am interested in studying animals and tend to notice beauty in details and in the world around me. I am drawn to psychological analysis, I am emotionally aware and empathetic, and I can deeply understand people and animals. I am also interested in mysticism and criminology, particularly in how the human mind works and the psychological mechanisms behind serious crimes.
